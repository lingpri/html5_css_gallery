Becoming

Michelle Obama

First Lady Michelle Obama describes what it was like growing up in the south side of Chicago, living with her parents, Aunt Robby, going to Princeton for her college, breaking the barriers that were always presented to her, marrying Barrack Obama before he was the forty-fourth President of United States of America. Sometimes, all she could do was buy winter hats for her two daughters and make sure they were safe.
